About Lathitha

Biography

My name is Lathitha Olothando Mzantsi, I was born on the 15th of August 2005 to my Mom Sibongile and Dad Thembekile. I am the last of my parents' four children. I was a happy baby growing up with my brothers and sister. I started school at Bouncing Bunny's Daycare and then proceeded to Central Primary School, where I did grades R-7.


I then went on to Hudson Park High School, where I discovered a passion for power lifting and biking. At Hudson, I made great friends and tried my best to be a good learner. I am generally liked by my teachers and peers. After high school, I plan to pursue my tertiary studies and pursue a prosperous career in real estate and property development.

For now, I am working hard to become a man with values and integrity. I seek to leave a lasting and positive impact on those around me. My daily goal is to cultivate the courage necessary to pursue my dreams. I want to be a man of substance. I want to continuously seek self-improvement. I want to be a man who never gives up. A man who is stubbornly resilient. I want to be good to those around me, to love my family, and to respect and honor my parents. Most importantly, I want to be a man who worships and fears God.


I will work hard all my life to be the best man I can be.
